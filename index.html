About

Professor Pawel Bilinski is the Director of the Centre for Financial Analysis and Reporting Research at Bayes Business School, City St Georges, University of London. He is also the Research Director for the Faculty of Finance. In 2024, he became Editor of the Journal of Business Finance and Accounting, and in 2026, he became Associate Editor at the British Accounting Review. In 2023, he was elected to the Board of the European Accounting Association as the National Representative for the United Kingdom (term 2023–2026).

He was the Course Director of the MSc in Corporate Finance and of the BSc Accounting and Finance at Bayes Business School. In 2016, he established and coordinated the Young Accounting Scholars Network. Before joining the Accounting Group at Bayes Business School, he held posts at Manchester Business School and at Lancaster University Management School. He held visiting positions at other institutions, including at the University of Bristol and Warwick Business School.

Pawel's research interests are in the area of accounting and finance and include firm disclosure policies, the properties of analyst research outputs, investment banking relationships, and empirical corporate finance.

He has published in top accounting and finance journals such as The Accounting Review, British Journal of Management, Journal of Banking and Finance, Journal of Business Finance and Accounting, Abacus, European Financial Management, and Financial Markets, Institutions, and Instruments. He is Editorial Board Member of the Journal of Business Finance and Accounting, Accounting and Business Research, European Accounting Review, and the Journal of Accounting, Finance and Economics.

His research has received funding from the 6th Marie Curie Framework Programme, the Malmsten Foundation, and Manchester Business School.

Pawel has experience teaching accounting (financial and managerial accounting) and finance courses at Executive, MBA, MSc, and undergraduate level in the UK, Hong Kong, Dubai, Shanghai, the Channel Islands and Saudi Arabia. He has received excellent feedback on his engaging and practice-oriented teaching.
